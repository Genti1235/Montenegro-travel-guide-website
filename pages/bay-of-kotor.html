Destination guide

Bay of Kotor

A dramatic bay of stone towns, calm water, steep mountains, and unforgettable viewpoints.

What to do first

Start in Kotor Old Town, walk the city walls if you have the energy, then continue around the bay to Perast for waterfront views and boat trips to Our Lady of the Rocks. The bay is compact, but the scenery changes constantly as the road bends around the water.

Best base

Kotor for old-town atmosphere, Perast for romance, Tivat for marina comfort.

Do not miss

Perast, Kotor Fortress, boat trips, seafood lunches, and sunset above the bay.
