7-Day Montenegro Road Trip Itinerary

A simple route connecting the coast, the Bay of Kotor, mountain national parks, river canyons, and lake country.

Day 1

Arrive on the coast

Settle in around Tivat, Kotor, or Budva. Keep the first evening easy with a waterfront dinner.

Day 2

Bay of Kotor

Explore Kotor Old Town, Perast, and the bay road. Add a viewpoint if the weather is clear.

Day 3

Lovcen and Cetinje

Drive the mountain road, visit Lovcen National Park, then continue to Montenegro's old royal capital.

Day 4

Skadar Lake

Take a boat trip, visit fishing villages, and slow the pace down before heading north.

Days 5-6

Durmitor and Tara Canyon

Base yourself near Zabljak for Black Lake, mountain walks, canyon viewpoints, and rafting if in season.

Day 7

Return south

Drive back toward Podgorica, Tivat, or the coast depending on your flight and final night.

Before you drive

Montenegro rewards a rental car, but roads can be narrow and mountain drives need patience. Read the transport guide before booking.
