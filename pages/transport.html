Buses and Car Rentals in Montenegro

Use this page to decide whether your trip works best by bus, rental car, taxi transfer, or a mix of all three.

Rental car

Best for mountain routes, national parks, photo stops, and flexible itineraries. Book early in summer.

Bus

Good for city-to-city travel on the coast and between larger towns. Check station times before relying on late connections.

Taxi or transfer

Useful for airports, short coastal hops, and routes where bus schedules are awkward.

Simple recommendation

If you are only visiting Kotor, Budva, Tivat, and nearby beaches, buses and transfers can work. If you want Durmitor, Tara Canyon, Lovcen, or Skadar Lake viewpoints, rent a car for at least part of the trip.
